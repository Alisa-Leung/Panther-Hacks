# Panther-Hacks
Panther Hacks is a hackathon taking place at Burlingame High School on Saturday, April 18th. We're excited to have you join us for the fun!
#
This website serves as the landing page for Burlingame High School's first ever hackathon! My team and I are excited to host students from across the Bay Area to participate in coding innovative solutions to real-world problems. I built this site with plain HTML, CSS, and JS with little add-ons over the course of ~1 month. I'm excited to see what happens as the date nears!
