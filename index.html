Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all! Our hackathon is open to both beginner and experienced coders. We'll have workshops and mentors to guide you along the way so that you can learn and get started on your project.

What should I bring?

Please bring your laptop, a charger, and anything else you feel you will need to succeed. Snacks and drinks are provided throughout the day along with lunch.

Do I need to work with a team?

Working with a team is not required; however, it's highly encouraged, as teamwork is what makes a hackathon fun! If you would like to be part of a team and aren't already, you will be matched with others at the event.
